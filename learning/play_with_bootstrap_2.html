What is Code Chef?

CodeChef promotes constant learning and friendly programming competitions for its users, along with the support of its large community of problem curators. CodeChef was created as a platform to help programmers make it big in the world of algorithms, computer programming, and programming contests.

About code chef contests

CodeChef was created as a community platform to help coders compete against each other and make it big in the world of computer programming. CodeChef conducts 8 or 9 rated coding competitions every month with participation from programmers all across the globe.
